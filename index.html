Back to the shack.

To put the revolutionary back into this whole "revolutionary technology" thing, we gather here, within the walls of these battered barracks, free software and the hacker mentality forever under attack, and record our words over media designed and implemented by people much like us.

Some essays presented are new, some have been passed down through the generations. Some are used by permission and at the author's request, others are used under licenses developed by likeminded individuals.

To contribute, simply do all the work that is represented in a basic pull request and pull. If your work does not conflict with the rest here, and if nobody objects, it will be posted. That is our submission process. Contact etc@barracks.tech if you have any questions.
